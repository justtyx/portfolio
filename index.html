Cases

1. BYÙ Studio

BYÙ Studio is a co-creation jewellery brand founded by a fellow KEA student. BYÙ Studio is yet to launch and me together with my team developed the whole digital presence and brand identity for it.

Our product consisted of visual identity, especially in terms of colours, logo and graphic elements. We also developed a social media strategy, introductionary video and e-commerce solution.

2. Circulents

Circulents are the future consumers and alumni of Leadership Programme in Circular Economy which I also took part in. As a final assignment, me and 14 people accross the Nordic and the Baltic sea region produced an eight-themed action plan with suggestions on how to shift towards circular future.

I believe that attractive communication is vital for spreading the message, thus I took a lead designer's role in turning a blank text document into a website, social media posts and an attractive PDF handbook.

3. People of 2050

I believe that it is extremely important to support non-governmental organisations. Thus, I have formed a partnership with a Copenhagen-based organisation focusing on educating the youth on climate change and sustainability - People of 2050.

During our ongoing collaboration I have produced several roll-ups for print as well as a handful of digital infopacks for participants.
