About me

Aside from being a multimedia designer with a focus on sustainability, I am a very proactive person.

I have a volunteered at 15 events, attended a myriad of international training courses on variety of topics such as European Parliament elections, circular economy, web development and so much more. I love meeting new people from all over the world, learning and discovering all the fun things in the world.

Recently, I have just started working on a long term project as one of the main project managers with a responsibility of developing strategy, digital communication and design. The project team aims to educate youth around the Nordic and the Baltic sea region on circular economy. We are called Circulents and we are a part of ReGeneration 2030.
